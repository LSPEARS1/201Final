About Me

Howdy! I'm a senior at Brigham Young University, navigating the exciting journey of both school and married life with my wonderful wife, Alyssa. We spend much of our time focusing on our faith in Jesus Christ and serving in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Growing up in rural Arkansas contributes to much of who I am - hunting, fishing, and spending time at my family farm are some of my favorite hobbies. This love for the environment complements my active lifestyle, where fitness plays a significant role, helping me stay grounded and focused.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I dedicate a substantial portion of my time to The Cougar Chronicle, a BYU independent student newspaper. Serving as the Head of Strategy, my role encompasses a variety of responsibilities. From planning engaging content and website design to enhancing our social media presence and crafting our outreach strategy, my goal is to ensure our newspaper resonates with and effectively reaches our student community. This role not only allows me to engage creatively but also to develop and refine my leadership and strategic planning skills.

My commitment to The Cougar Chronicle recently led to a proud moment at the Intercollegiate Studies Institute's Editor's Conference, where Jacob Christensen, our Editor-in-Chief, and I were honored to accept an award for Best New Publication. This recognition is a testament to our team's hard work and dedication.

Academically, I've honed a skill set in data analysis, with proficiency in Stata, Excel, and Tableau, alongside a foundational understanding of HTML. These skills not only enrich my academic and professional experiences but also fuel my passion for continuous learning and growth.
